You are looking at an experimental version of a mobile application designed for a final year IT subject. In reality, this mobile applicaiton will be able to locate you and suggest a room that you may be near. In this prototype, it will just choose a random room on campus.

You can use this prototype to enter any room on campus and check which classes will be in that room today. It actually works pretty well, at least for something that we came up with in our spare time.

Please keep in mind that it is a prototype, so its quality is going to be pretty low.
